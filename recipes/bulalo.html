Bulalo

Bulalo Soup

Bulalo is a popular Filipino soup dish that is typically made with beef shank and bone marrow, along with various vegetables like corn on the cob, bok choy, and potatoes.

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 lbs beef shank with bone marrow
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-3 corn on the cob, cut into 2-3 inch pieces
  • 2-3 potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 1 bunch bok choy or pechay, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Water, enough to cover the beef shank and vegetables

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, add the beef shank and enough water to cover it. Bring to a boil and skim off any impurities that rise to the surface.
  2. Add the chopped onion and garlic to the pot, along with some salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Lower the heat to a simmer and let the beef shank cook for about 2-3 hours, or until the meat is tender and falls off the bone.
  4. Once the beef shank is cooked, remove it from the pot and set it aside. Strain the broth to remove any bits and pieces.
  5. Return the broth to the pot and add the corn on the cob and potatoes. Let the vegetables cook for about 10-15 minutes, or until they are tender.
  6. Add the bok choy or pechay to the pot and let it cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  7. Add the beef shank back to the pot and let it heat up again.
  8. Season with more salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve hot with steamed rice.
